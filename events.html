Neighborhood Plan

As representatives of the businesses of our District, the Chamber is participting in the development and strategies for the Redruth Neighborhood Plan. Cooperation and partnership is paramount in planning for change and the future our community and District.

The plan includes but is not limited to

  • Economic Growth
  • Housing
  • Health and Education
  • Heritage
  • Transport
