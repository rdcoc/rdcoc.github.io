Our Featured Members

Krowji is Cornwall’s largest creative hub, providing studios, workspaces, meeting rooms, The Melting Pot Cafe and other services for a diverse range of creative businesses.

It’s home to a vibrant creative community which currently includes painters, jewellers, furniture makers, ceramicists, textile artists, web designers, theatre companies and musicians as well as several of Cornwall’s key creative sector agencies such as Creative Skills, FEAST, Carn to Cove and the Cornwall 365 Project, all of which are part of umbrella organisation Creative Kernow. Krowji welcomes all those involved in cultural work: from creative practitioners to organisations that provide services to creative businesses. With a uniquely wide spectrum of work created by its tenants, Krowji has a powerful collective impact quite unlike any other. A connected, supported, professional and above all creative and vibrant working environment, Krowji is constantly driving forward the exciting evolution of the Cornish arts and cultural sectors.


THURSTAN HOSKIN SOLICITORS is an independent law firm which has been part of the Cornish legal landscape for over three decades. The firm’s main office, in Redruth, situated in the centre of West Cornwall, was established in 1979, conveniently sited close to the town centre, the railway station and the main A30. We have recently opened a new base in St Agnes. The founder of the firm, Thurstan Hoskin, was born and raised in the county and we take pride in our Cornish roots. We act for clients based across the length and breadth of the county, ‘up country’ as well as abroad.

Our main areas of work are: • Property Services • Wills, Probate, Trusts and Powers of Attorney • Matrimonial and Children Law • Dispute Resolution

The Craft Collective is an Arts and Crafts Gift shop, where you can find that perfect and unique present for yourself or your loved ones. We offer crafts people the opportunity to be able to showcase and sell their artwork or craftsmanship by renting a space from us. We then market, and sell their products and return to them 100% of the sales. This is a collective of artists and crafts people who have come together to promote the creative industries. With that in mind we also offer a great range of workshops to enable people to get creative, learn something knew and develop their talents.

Established in 1984 Cornish Riding Holidays/Wheal Buller Riding School is a family run business open all the year around for lessons, hacking and fully inclusive riding courses. We cater for all ages and abilities, groups or individuals you can come for a one off ride around Carn Brea Castle, a lesson or hack or work towards a professional qualification. Having around 50 horses and ponies from quiet weight carrying cobs to competition horses there is one to suit everyone. All our friendly, helpful staff are qualified, First Aid trained and CRB checked.

We take beginners who want to go sedately trekking and enjoy the stunning Cornwall scenery and riders who are capable of faster more exciting hacking. Why not come along and have a go? You could start with a riding lesson indoors or go out along the World Heritage Site of the Mineral Tramways.

We strive to bring the community together, strengthen our business to business relationships and promote the District through various methods.

Our Meetings

The Chamber meets on the first Thursday of each month in the Redruth Town Council Chambers. Meeting starts at 18:00. Each month we will have n open discussion of concerns, suggestions and ideas. We will also conduct workshops and seminars to help our local businesses with issues, digital media, social media and web site design and implementation.
